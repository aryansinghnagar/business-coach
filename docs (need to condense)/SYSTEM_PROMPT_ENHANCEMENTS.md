The SYSTEM_PROMPT has been significantly expanded from ~180 lines to ~320 lines, transforming it from a good business coach into an elite, comprehensive business meeting advisor with deep expertise across multiple domains.

### 1. Expanded Core Identity
**Before**: Basic business coach description
**After**: Elite advisor with:
- Master-level expertise across multiple domains
- Experience across Fortune 500, startups, government, international organizations
- Exceptional emotional intelligence
- Strategic and tactical precision

## Usage Examples

The enhanced prompt will provide guidance like:

**Before**: "Ask a question to re-engage"
**After**: "Directly address [Name] with a specific question about [Topic] - their engagement dropped when [Topic] was mentioned. Use their name and make eye contact. Consider: '[Name], I'd value your perspective on [specific aspect] - how do you see this impacting [their area]?' This personalizes the question and shows you value their input."

**Before**: "Present your proposal"
**After**: "This is your optimal moment - engagement is at 85% and [Participant] just asked a clarifying question showing interest. Present your key proposal now using the 'Problem-Solution-Benefit' structure: (1) Acknowledge their concern about [X], (2) Present your solution addressing [X], (3) Highlight the specific benefit to [their interest]. Close with: 'Would you be open to exploring this further?' This structure addresses their concern while advancing your proposal."
